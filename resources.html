Resources

Here's a fantastically super list of Canadian and international GLBT resources. Check 'em out!

Resources by Region

Alberta Resources

  • Calgary Outlink
    Provides a safe community space equipped with support services, education opportunities, a crisis line, and access to resources.
  • Camp Fyrefly
    A leadership retreat for GLBT youth.
  • GALAP
    Serving the GLBT community of greater Grande Prairie for over ten years.
  • OUTreach
    The University of Alberta’s GLBT student group
  • Pride Centre of Edmonton
    Provides resources, support and programming for the GLBT community in Edmonton and surrounding areas.
  • Queers On Campus
    The University of Calgary’s GLBT student group
  • The Altview Foundation
    Provides resources and support to GLBT persons in Strathcona County and surrounding regions.

British Columbia Resources

  • Chilliwack Pride
    Promoting a greater understanding of lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender issues in the community of Chilliwack.
  • Pride Education Network
    Promotes a safe, respectful and inclusive learning environment for all.
  • Qmunity
    A Vancouver-based GLBT resource centre.
  • Trans Alliance Society
    Transgender support, education, outreach and advocacy for the BC area.

Canada-wide Resources

  • Egale Canada
    A national organization that advances equality for Canada’s GLBT citizens.
  • Lambda Foundation
    A national series of scholarships in gay and lesbian studies.
  • PFLAG Canada
    A support group for parents and friends of gays and lesbians, with multiple local chapters.
  • PFLAG Vancouver
    Provides support for all family members and friends in the Vancouver region.
  • Pink Shirt Day
    An anti-bullying event held in schools across Canada
  • Rainbow Link
    Helps donate GLBT-themed books to libraries across Canada.

International Resources

  • BGIOK
    A UK-based site providing information and advice for GLBT youth under twenty-five.
  • GLSEN
    An American-based organisation devoted to ensuring safe schools for GLBT youth.
  • Human Rights Campaign
    Working for GLBT rights in the United States.
  • Soulforce
    Committed to freedom for lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and queer people from religious and political oppression through relentless nonviolent resistance.
  • Truth Wins Out
    Fighting anti-gay lies and the ex-gay myth.

Manitoba Resources

  • Rainbow Resource Centre
    Serving Manitoba’s Gay, Lesbian, Bisexual, Transgender, and Two-Spirit Communities

New Brunswick Resources

  • Pride in Education
    Pride in Education is a provincial committee that was started in 2008 by teachers concerned about the well-being and safety of sexual minority students in New Brunswick.

Nova Scotia Resources

  • Allies at Dalhousie
    The Dalhousie University GLBT student association.
  • DalOUT
    The LGBTQ Society of Dalhousie University
  • Nova Scotia Rainbow Action Project
    A non-profit society working for GLBT Nova Scotians.
  • The Youth Project
    A GLBT resource centre in downtown Halifax.

Ontario Resources

  • GLOW
    The Queer and Questioning Community Centre at the University of Waterloo.
  • OK2BEME
    Support services for GLBT youth in the Waterloo region.
  • Ontario Rainbow Alliance of the Deaf
    Serving the deaf, hard of hearing, and hearing GLBT community in Ontario.
  • Out in Kingston
    GLBT community listings for the Kingston region.
  • Rainbow Service
    Promoting, advocating, and celebrating the interests of the GLBT community in Ontario.
  • RyePride
    The Ryerson University GLBT students association.
  • The Rainbow Youth Coalition
    Making safer spaces for queer youth in the counties of Peterborough, Northumberland, City of Kawartha Lakes and Haliburton.
  • Xpressions
    Toronto-based trangender support and social group.

Québec Resources

  • CAEO Québec
    GLBT support and resources for the English-speaking GLBT community in Québec.
  • CCGLM
    The Montreal Gay and Lesbian Community Centre
  • Coalition gaie et lesbienne du Québec
    Un organisme qui représente les droits de la communauté gaie et lesbienne du Québec.
  • Gai Écoute
    Centre d’aide, d’écoute téléphonique et renseignements des gais du Québec.
  • GRIS Montréal
    Un organisme qui offre des services de démystification de l’homosexualité aux jeunes en milieu scolaire.
  • LGBTQ Youth Centre
    A GLBT support centre for youth in Montréal’s West Island.

Saskatchewan Resources

  • Camp Fyrefly Saskatchewan
    The Saskatchewan division of Camp Fyrefly, a leadership retreat for GLBT youth.
  • UR Pride Centre
    A non-profit GLBT service provider for the University of Regina.
